FAQ

What tax inputs are included?

The calculator estimates U.S. federal income tax using filing status and the standard deduction. It can also include FICA.

Does the tool save my salary?

No. Inputs stay in your browser session and are not stored by the site.

Why are there no state taxes here?

This first version keeps scope narrow so the output stays simple and deterministic. State taxes vary by jurisdiction and are excluded.
